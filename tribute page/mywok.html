Koffi Annan

Koffi Annan was a man like no other.He dedicated his life to bring about a noble change in Africa.

Here's a life summary of Koffi Annan:

  • 1938-Koffi Annan was born in Kumasi,Ghana
  • 1957-Graduated from Mfantsipim Methodist boarding school.He went thereafter for further education at Kumasi College of science and technology,Macalester College in St.Paul,Minnesota and Graduate institute of International Studies in Geneva,Switzerland.
  • 1962-Became a budget officer for the World Health Organisation which is a sector of the UN
  • (1974-1976)-He took a short break from working in WHO and worked as a tourism director in his home country Ghana.
  • (1987-1996)-He was appointed assistant secretary general in three consecutive positions ie:Human Resources Management & Security coordinator,Program Planning,budget & Finance and Controller and finally Peacekeeping operations
  • (1994-1995)-He was under the secretary general
  • 1997-This is the lifechanging year when he was appointed secretary general after being recommended by the World Health Organisation
  • 2001-He was given the Nobel Prize Award for his outstanding work in helping to create a better organised and peaceful world.
  • 2006-He retired from the UN and started helping out in different organisations with a global focus.
  • He was appointed as UN Arab League envoy to Syria to try and end civil war taking place there though he later resigned.
  • 2018-He died after a short-term illness.
