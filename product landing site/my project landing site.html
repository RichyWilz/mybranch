About us

Kayiira Bakery is a Ugandan based company that deals in production and distribution of yummy appetising confectionary. These include cakes, cookies,daddies and bread. Established in 2017, Kayiira Bakery continues to produce world class confectionary at ever affordable prices. We remain committed to making new and delicious recipes for mouth-watering goodies.

Quality Assurance

Our products are UNBS certified as customer satisfaction is number one priority at Kayiira Bakery. The process of making our products right from pre to post production is done by extreme professionals.The products and staff are constantly monitored and supervised respectively.
Therefore,we as Kayiira Bakery only offer quality foodstuffs that are guaranteed to leave you satsified.
